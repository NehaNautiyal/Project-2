Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I make a bet?
    • Create an account
    • Click the “New Bet” button in the navbar
    • Enter the Terms, Amount, End Date, and challenge somebody, either through their username or an email or social media message
    • If they accept, you’ve got a bet!
  2. How do we decide who wins a bet?
    • When your end date arrives, both betters will receive a notification that they need to rule on their bet.
    • Both users will decide on the winner of the bet. If both agree, the bet is completed.
    • If both users disagree, they will have to nominate a mediator. The mediator will receive an extra fee from the loser.
    • If the users are unable to agree on a mediator, they will both receive 50% of their points back, with the rest going into the daily raffle.
  3. What is the daily raffle?

    Any time points are inserted back into the system, they will be added to a daily raffle total. Each day, you can guess how many points are in the raffle. The closest guess wins all the points!

  4. Why can’t I bet more points?

    To begin, you have a cap on the amount you can wager in each bet. As you invite friends, make and mediate bets, and participate in the marketplace, your cap will grow and you can wager more!

  5. What can I get with my points?
    • When you reach total point milestones, you can trade points in for social media clout!
    • iBetya will offer points to other users to follow you on your choice of social media platforms.
    • So start betting, get sharing, and build your brand with iBetya!
